About EveOne

EveOne is a event management app for schools. It is designed to bridge the communication gap between schools and parents

We automate managing events/tournaments (such as speech and debate) for schools where parent participate for judging events. Our approach is to facilitate collaboration between organizer schools and parents in a seamless fashion
